Learning Corner

Digital Nagrik

  • Understand your digital rights and responsibilities.
  • Use technology ethically and respect others' privacy.
  • Stay informed about digital policies and regulations.
  • Contribute to a safe and secure digital environment.
  • Promote digital literacy and inclusivity.

Cyber Safety Tips

  • Keep your software up to date.
  • Use strong passwords and change them regularly.
  • Be wary of unsolicited emails or messages asking for personal information.
  • Enable two-factor authentication for an extra layer of security.
  • Regularly back up your data to prevent loss in case of a cyber attack.

Cyber Awareness

  • Be cautious of phishing emails.
  • Avoid sharing personal information online.
  • Keep personal and work devices separate to protect corporate data.
  • Educate yourself about the latest cyber scams and how to avoid them.
  • Promote a culture of security within your organization and among peers.

Report Financial Fraud

If you suspect any financial fraud or cybercrime, report it immediately to help prevent further damage.
